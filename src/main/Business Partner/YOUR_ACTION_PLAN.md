## 🎯 THE 5-STEP FRAMEWORK (MEMORIZE!)

For EVERY question they ask, use this:

```
STEP 1: CLARIFY
  "Let me ask some clarifying questions..."
  Ask 3-4 questions to understand the problem

STEP 2: DATA PROBLEM
  "So the data problem is..."
  Identify what data you need

STEP 3: SOLUTION
  "I'd propose..."
  Explain your approach

STEP 4: COLLABORATION
  "I'd work with teams by..."
  Show you work cross-functionally

STEP 5: IMPACT
  "This would help us by..."
  Connect to business outcome
```
